Words You Today

Words You Today is a free app that helps you keep track of who you are.

The app is in development. One person (me) is creating it: Dr. Jason Jeffrey Jones

Read the Founding Statement.

How it works

You see randomly chosen words and answer the question: Does this word describe me today?

Swipe left to answer No or swipe right for Yes.

Swipe words everyday, and the app can fulfill its purpose: help you keep track of who you are and who you were!
